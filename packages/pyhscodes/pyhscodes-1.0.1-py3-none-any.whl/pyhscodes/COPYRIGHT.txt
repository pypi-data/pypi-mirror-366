HS Codes Data:
- Data in the /databases/ folder contains Harmonized System (HS) codes from 
  the World Customs Organization (WCO). The HS codes are an international 
  standard for classifying traded products, maintained by the WCO.

- The HS code data is provided for informational purposes. Users should verify 
  current codes with official WCO publications or national customs authorities 
  for authoritative classification.
